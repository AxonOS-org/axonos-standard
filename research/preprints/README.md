# Research Preprints

This directory contains non-normative research artifacts related to AxonOS.

Preprints are useful evidence and design context, but they are not themselves
the AxonOS Standard. Normative requirements must be adopted through the standard
and RFC process.

## Included artifacts

| File | Status | Role |
|---|---|---|
| `axonos-microkernel-analytical-preprint-2026-05.pdf` | Preprint; not peer-reviewed | Analytical microkernel design, schedulability model, capability isolation, and falsifiable predictions |

## Interpretation

The included preprint is analytical. It should be read as design evidence and
research context, not as a final conformance specification or regulatory claim.

Future arXiv, Zenodo, or peer-reviewed versions should be added here with clear
version labels and citations.
